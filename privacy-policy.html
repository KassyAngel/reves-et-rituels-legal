4Data Collected

The following data is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • Your preferred language (French / English)
  • Your dream journal entries
  • Your notification preferences (reminder time, enabled/disabled)
  • Your browsing history of symbols and rituals

✨ Important: This data remains solely on your device. If you uninstall the application or delete the data, this information will be permanently lost.

4.2 Data collected for the Premium version

Only if you subscribe to the Premium version:

  • RevenueCat user identifier: For purchase management via Google Play
  • Subscription and expiry date: To manage the validity of your Premium access
  • Payment status: Payment confirmation via Google Play Billing
  • Subscription type: Monthly (€3.99/month), Annual (€23.99/year) or Lifetime (€49.99)

4.3 Technical data collected automatically

  • Device type and operating system

  • Anonymised app usage data
